About us

The Department of Electrical Systems Engineering at the Faculty of Technology, University of M'Hamed Bougara Boumerdes, Algeria, was established to meet the growing demands of the electrical and energy sectors. It plays a crucial role in providing high-quality education and research opportunities in fields such as power generation, electrical networks, and electronics. The department is equipped with advanced laboratories and state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that students receive both theoretical and practical training. It aims to prepare graduates to excel in various industrial and academic environments, contributing to the technological development of Algeria and beyond.

Established as part of the larger Faculty of Technology, the Department of Electrical Systems Engineering has developed strong collaborations with national and international industries, energy companies, and research institutions. These partnerships allow students to gain real-world experience through internships and projects, ensuring they are well-prepared for the challenges of modern electrical engineering. The department also offers a wide range of specializations, including renewable energy systems, smart grids, electrical machines, and power electronics, aligning its programs with the latest trends in the global energy and technology sectors.

The department is committed to fostering a research-driven environment, encouraging both faculty and students to engage in innovative projects and advanced studies. With a focus on sustainable energy solutions, the department aims to contribute significantly to the national energy strategy, particularly in renewable energy and energy efficiency. The University of M'Hamed Bougara Boumerdes supports these initiatives by promoting interdisciplinary research and innovation. A dedicated team of faculty members and industry experts guide the department, ensuring it remains at the forefront of electrical systems engineering education in Algeria.
